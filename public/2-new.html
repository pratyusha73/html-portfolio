Ghibli Movies

My top 3 movies:

Howl's moving castle

The soundtrack along with the chaotically beautiful art make this movie the current holder of the number 1 position.
Additionally, the storyline and the characters are all well-written

Porco Rosso

One of the lesser appreciated movies by the studio. This has a compelling story and an ending that left me satisfied

From Up On The Poppy Hill

The imagery, artwork and soundtrack leave the viewer feeling content. Coming of age movie done in Studio Ghibli's style.
